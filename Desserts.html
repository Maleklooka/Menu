Desserts

    Cheesecake

    Cheesecake is a rich, creamy dessert made primarily from cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and a crust.


  • Classic Cheesecake (7 Dinar):

    A Classic Cheesecake is a traditional dessert that typically features a rich, creamy filling made primarily from cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and vanilla, all set on a cookie or biscuit crust. It's known for its smooth, dense texture and slightly tangy flavor, thanks to the cream cheese.

    Ingridiants:

    Rich cream cheese filling with vanilla, eggs, and sugar, set on a buttery graham cracker crust.

  • Chocolate Cheesecake (8 Dinar):

    A Chocolate Cheesecake is a variation of classic cheesecake, where chocolate is incorporated into the filling or added as a topping to enhance the flavor.

    Ingridiants:

    Rich cream cheese filling with dark chocolate, eggs, sugar, and vanilla, on a chocolate cookie crust, topped with a smooth chocolate ganache.

  • Strawberry Cheesecake (8 Dinar):

    A Strawberry Cheesecake is a delicious variation of classic cheesecake that features a rich, creamy filling made from cream cheese and sugar, topped with a layer of fresh strawberries or strawberry compote.

    Ingridiants:

    Smooth cream cheese filling with vanilla and sugar, set on a graham cracker crust, topped with fresh strawberries and a sweet strawberry glaze.

  • Caramel Cheesecake (8 Dinar):

    A Caramel Cheesecake is a decadent variation of classic cheesecake, featuring a rich, creamy filling made from cream cheese and sugar, with the addition of caramel to enhance the flavor.

    Ingridiants:

    Rich cream cheese filling with a hint of caramel, set on a buttery graham cracker crust, drizzled with caramel sauce, and topped with whipped cream and chopped pecans.

  • Blueberry Cheesecake (8 Dinar):

    A Blueberry Cheesecake is a delightful variation of the classic cheesecake, where the rich, creamy filling is complemented by the sweet and slightly tart flavor of blueberries.

    Ingridiants:

    Cream cheese filling with vanilla and fresh blueberries, set on a graham cracker crust, topped with blueberry compote and a dollop of whipped cream.

    Flan

    Flan is a classic, rich, and creamy dessert made from a custard base, typically topped with caramel sauce. It's a popular dessert in many countries, especially in Latin America, Spain, and parts of Asia. Flan is also known by other names, such as crème caramel (in French cuisine) and leche flan (in some Spanish-speaking countries). Flan is typically made by baking the custard in a water bath (also called a bain-marie) to create a smooth, silky texture. The caramel is either poured into the bottom of the pan before adding the custard or created separately and poured on top after baking. Once the flan is baked, it's usually chilled to allow the flavors to meld together and for the caramel to set.


  • Classic Flan (6 Dinar):

    Classic flan, also known as crème caramel, is a traditional custard dessert made with eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract, topped with a layer of caramel sauce.

    Ingridiants:

    A smooth, velvety custard made with eggs, whole milk, sugar, and vanilla extract, topped with a golden, silky caramel sauce.

  • Strawberry Flan (7 Dinar):

    A Strawberry Flan is a delightful variation of the classic flan (or crème caramel) dessert, infused with the fresh, fruity flavor of strawberries.

    Ingridiants:

    Silky, smooth custard made with eggs, whole milk, sugar, and vanilla extract, blended with pureed strawberries for a refreshing twist, topped with fresh strawberries and a sweet strawberry glaze.

  • Matcha Flan (10 Dinar):

    Matcha Flan is a creative variation of the classic flan (or crème caramel) that incorporates matcha powder, which is a finely ground, powdered form of green tea.

    Ingridiants:

    A silky smooth custard made with eggs, whole milk, sugar, and vanilla extract, delicately flavored with matcha green tea powder, and finished with a sweet caramel sauce.

  • Lime Flan (7 Dinar):

    Lime Flan is a refreshing variation of the classic flan (or crème caramel) that incorporates the bright, tangy flavor of lime into the custard base.

    Ingridiants:

    Silky smooth custard made with eggs, whole milk, sugar, and vanilla extract, delicately flavored with fresh lime juice and lime zest, finished with a golden caramel sauce.

  • Blueberry Flan (7 Dinar):

    Blueberry Flan is a delicious variation of the classic flan (or crème caramel) that incorporates the sweet, slightly tart flavor of blueberries into the custard.

    Ingridiants:

    A silky smooth custard made with eggs, whole milk, sugar, and vanilla extract, delicately flavored with pureed blueberries, and finished with a sweet blueberry sauce.
