Main Course

    Pasta

    Pasta is a traditional Italian dish made from durum wheat semolina or flour mixed with water (and sometimes eggs), then formed into various shapes and sizes before being cooked by boiling.


  • Chicken Alfredo (20 Dinar):

    Chicken Alfredo is a dish typically made up of chicken, pasta (often fettuccine), and Alfredo sauce, served alongside.

    Ingridiants:

    Grilled chicken, fettuccine pasta, creamy Alfredo sauce made with butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese.

  • Spaghetti & Meatballs (24 Dinar):

    Spaghetti & Meatballs is a classic Italian-American dish consisting of spaghetti pasta served with meatballs and typically topped with a tomato-based sauce.

    Ingridiants:

    Spaghetti pasta, homemade beef and pork meatballs, served with a rich tomato marinara sauce, and topped with grated Parmesan cheese.

  • Classic Lasagna (28 Dinar):

    Classic Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made from layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and béchamel (white sauce).

    Ingridiants:

    Layers of lasagna noodles, seasoned ground beef, rich marinara sauce, creamy béchamel, ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan, baked to perfection.

  • Tagliatelle With Bacon & Mushrooms (22 Dinar):

    Tagliatelle with Bacon & Mushrooms is a delicious Italian pasta dish featuring tagliatelle (a type of long, flat pasta) combined with bacon (or pancetta) and mushrooms, typically served with a savory sauce.

    Ingridiants:

    Tagliatelle pasta, pancetta, tender mushrooms, sautéed with garlic, finished with a rich cream sauce, and topped with grated Parmesan.

  • Spaghetti Carbonara (19 Dinar):

    Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish that originated in Rome. It's a simple yet flavorful dish made with spaghetti pasta, a creamy egg-based sauce, cheese, and pancetta.

    Ingridiants:

    Traditional Roman-style spaghetti with pancetta, eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper in a rich, creamy sauce.

    Pizza

    Pizza is a popular Italian dish that consists of a round, flat base of dough topped with a variety of ingredients. The base is usually made of wheat flour, water, yeast, and salt, and is baked in an oven until it becomes golden and crispy.


  • Margherita Pizza (15 Dinar):

    Margherita Pizza is a traditional and classic Italian pizza, known for its simple ingredients and elegant flavors.

    Ingridiants:

    Classic tomato sauce, creamy fresh mozzarella, fresh basil, extra virgin olive oil, and a golden crispy crust.

  • Pepperoni Pizza (18 Dinar):

    Pepperoni Pizza is a popular type of pizza that is known for its savory, spicy topping of pepperoni, which is a type of dry-cured, spiced sausage made from beef. It is one of the most beloved pizza varieties, especially in the United States, and is commonly featured on menus worldwide.

    Ingridiants:

    Classic tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and spicy pepperoni slices on a golden, crispy crust.

  • Hawaii Pizza (20 Dinar):

    Hawaiian Pizza is a popular style of pizza known for its combination of pineapple and ham as toppings.

    Ingridiants:

    Classic tomato sauce, melted mozzarella, savory ham, and sweet pineapple on a golden, crispy crust.

  • Meat Lovers Pizza (27 Dinar):

    Meat Lovers Pizza is a popular style of pizza that features a variety of meats as its main toppings. It's known for being hearty and filling, often favored by those who enjoy savory, protein-packed dishes. The combination of different types of meats adds a rich and flavorful taste to the pizza.

    Ingridiants:

    Classic tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, savory sausage, crispy bacon, tender ham, and seasoned ground beef on a golden, crispy crust.

  • Pesto Pizza (16 Dinar):

    Pesto Pizza is a delicious and flavorful pizza that uses pesto sauce as the base instead of traditional tomato sauce.

    Ingridiants:

    Fresh basil pesto, melted mozzarella, and your choice of additional toppings such as grilled chicken, tomatoes, arugula, or pine nuts, on a crispy, golden crust.
